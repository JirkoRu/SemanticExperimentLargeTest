INSTRUCTIONS

Thank you for participating in our study!

In this study, you will be seeing different classes of images. Your task is to learn the properties associated with each class of images. The better you are at predicting the properties of each class, the higher your bonus payment will be.

The task consist of multiple blocks. After each block is finished you will see a blank screen and you can choose take a short break at this time.

This experiment should take about 60 min. This task requires a lot of attention. Please make sure you are undisturbed for the next 60 minutes.

Please note:
(1) You will need a keyboard and a mouse to participate in this study.
(2) This task will not work on a phone or tablet.
(3) This task also does not work with Internet Explorer.

After the last block, it might show a white screen and nothing else. Please be patient! The data is being sent. If it takes longer than 10 minutes and you don't receive your verification code, send us an email (hiplab@psy.ox.ac.uk).
